My name is Jiaxin Zhang from China, and currently I am a PhD student of The University of Strathclyde, supervised by Dr.Yashar Moshfeghi and Professor Crawford Revie. I finished my Master Degree in The University of Sheffield, supervised by Dr.Andreas Vlachos, and my Bachelor Degree from Chang'an University. Before my PhD time, I spent one and half a year working in TCL Corporate Research Co., Ltd as a NLP Algorithm Engineer.

My research interset is around Natural Language Procssing, particularly in generating description from the table format data, and guiding human to have a satisfied conversation according to the table information with the agent system. Hopefully, I will add more and more research work during my PhD area.
